About Us

In the heart of Kandy, where the breezy hills cradle our beloved city, BreezyBite Bakers first took root in 1980. Over the past 40 years, we've evolved from a cozy neighborhood bak0ery serving local homes and school-goers into a thriving establishment cherished for our artisanal bread, traditional buns, and signature piano roll buns.

Our journey has been one of resilience and expansion. From a small-scale bakery, we grew into a network of bustling outlets, delivering a diverse array of freshly baked delights from our central facility. In the face of new challenges posed by industry giants in the 2000s, such as KFC, Pizza Hut, and Burger King, we not only stood our ground but emerged stronger.

Today, BreezyBite Bakers stands as a testament to our commitment to quality and innovation. With a wide-ranging menu that reflects our dedication to culinary excellence, we invite you to savor the unique flavors that define our bakery. As we step into the digital age, our focus on growth and accessibility takes form through our website, providing you with the convenience of online ordering.

BreezyBite Bakers, where the winds of tradition meet the modern aroma of freshly baked dreams. Join us on this delectable journey!

Our Vision

At BreezyBite Bakers, our vision is more than a statement; it's a guiding light that shapes our identity and propels us forward.

Beloved Neighborhood Bakery

We envision becoming the heartbeat of our community, a beloved neighborhood bakery that holds a special place in the hearts of those we serve. Our dedication to excellence in baking and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction set the foundation for this cherished status.

Dedication to Excellence and Innovation

We are driven by a dual commitment – a dedication to excellence in our traditional baking practices and an unyielding pursuit of innovation. We strive to blend the time-honored art of baking with a spirit of constant evolution, ensuring that our offerings remain both timeless and cutting-edge.

Expanding Reach, Strengthening Bonds

As we aspire to be the cornerstone of our neighborhood, we also look beyond, envisioning a broader reach. We seek to expand our presence, reaching new communities and diverse audiences. With each expansion, we aim not only to share our delectable creations but also to forge meaningful connections and contribute to the fabric of the communities we enter.

Continual Innovation

Innovation is the lifeblood of our vision. We embrace change, welcoming new flavors, techniques, and ideas into our repertoire. Through continual innovation, we aim to surprise and delight our customers, keeping their taste buds intrigued and excited with every visit.

Community Contributions

Our vision extends beyond our bakery walls. We see ourselves as contributors to the well-being of the communities we serve. Whether through supporting local initiatives, engaging in sustainable practices, or participating in community events, we aim to be an active and positive force for the betterment of our surroundings.

Delightful and Unforgettable Experiences

Ultimately, our vision is to create more than a bakery – we strive to craft delightful and unforgettable experiences.
