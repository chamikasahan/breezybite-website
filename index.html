Our Story

Established in 1980 near Kandy, our humble bakery has grown over four decades. Starting as a local favorite, we specialized in bread, buns, and more. Success led to multiple locations and a sizable central bakery. In the face of new challenges in the 2000s, we expanded our menu and persevered. Today, we offer a diverse range of delectable items. Embracing the digital era, we've built a user-friendly website for online ordering, aiming to reach a wider audience.

About Us

Our
Mission

To passionately craft and deliver exceptional baked goods that delight the senses, create lasting memories, and bring joy to every moment. We are committed to using the finest ingredients, maintaining impeccable quality, and fostering a warm, inviting environment for our customers.

Vision

To be the beloved neighborhood bakery, known for our dedication to excellence in baking and customer satisfaction. We aspire to expand our reach, while continually innovating and contributing to the communities we serve. Our vision is to create a delightful and unforgettable bakery experience for everyone who walks through our doors.
